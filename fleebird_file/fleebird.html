How Robo Flee Bird Works

1

Bird Detection

Advanced motion and ultrasonic sensors detect bird presence on power lines.

2

Activation

Laser and sound deterrents trigger to safely encourage birds to leave.

3

Cable Movement

Robot moves along the line to cover the area and prevent re-perching.

4

Solar Recharge

Returns to charging station or enters standby mode when not active.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Robo Flee Bird?

Robo Flee Bird is an autonomous robot designed to safely deter birds from power lines using non-harmful methods like gentle movement, sound frequencies, and laser pointers.

How does it deter birds safely?

It uses a combination of movement along the line, ultrasonic frequencies that birds find uncomfortable, and harmless laser patterns that encourage them to relocate without causing harm.

Is it safe for power lines?

Yes, the robot is designed with lightweight materials and secure attachment mechanisms that won't damage power lines or interfere with electrical currents.

Can it operate on solar power alone?

The robot is primarily solar-powered with battery backup for continuous operation during low-light conditions or at night.
